Jagruti Kanodia

Founder — Emotional Gym

Jagruti Kanodia is the founder of Emotional Gym and the architect behind its structured emotional training methodology. She created Emotional Gym to redefine emotional development as an active, trainable practice rather than a reactive process. Her work integrates emotional intelligence, experiential facilitation, and structured frameworks to help individuals build measurable emotional capacity. By positioning emotional growth as a discipline similar to physical training, Jagruti is pioneering a new category — emotional fitness — where individuals strengthen emotional awareness through guided practice and structured experiences.
